Distribution: The Ghatsila Copper Mine, located in the East Singhbhum district, is one of the notable copper mines in Jharkhand.
Environmental Impact: Copper mining has the potential to cause environmental harm if not managed carefully. The extraction and processing of copper ore can lead to soil and water contamination due to the release of heavy metals and toxic chemicals. Implementing stringent environmental regulations and adopting eco-friendly practices can mitigate these impacts.
